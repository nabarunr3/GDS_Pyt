	5' ATTGACTGACGATACGGATCGGAG 3' 
	3' TAACTGACTGCTATGCCTAGCCTC 5'

Forward frames:
Three frames for forward strand:

	5' ATTGACTGACGATACGGATCGGAG 3'

	ATT GAC TGA CGA TAC GGA TCG GAG

	AT TGA CTG ACG ATA CGG ATC GGA G

	A TTG ACT GAC GAT ACG GAT CGG AG 

Reverse frames:
The reverse frames are the 3 frames for the reverse strand

	5' CTCCGATCCGTATCGTCAGTCAAT 3'

	CTC CGA TCC GTA TCG TCA GTC AAT

	CT CCG ATC CGT ATC GTC AGT CAA T

	C TCC GAT CCG TAT CGT CAG TCA AT
